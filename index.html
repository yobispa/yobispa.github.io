.About me

Hi, I'm Yoandel Obispa! I am a software developer who is passionate about creating intuitive and user-friendly interfaces. I specialize in PHP, MySQL, JavaScript, HTML, and CSS, and I am highly skilled in using Bootstrap 5 and Symfony to design responsive and scalable web applications.
